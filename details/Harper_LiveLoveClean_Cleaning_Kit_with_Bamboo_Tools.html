Harper's Eco-Friendly Cleaning Kit

Harper's Live.Love.Clean. Cleaning Kit is designed with sustainability and convenience in mind. Made from all-natural bamboo, this versatile set includes a broom, a dust mop, two mop pads, a counter brush, and a debris pan. Perfect for keeping your home clean while reducing your environmental footprint.

  • All-natural bamboo cleaning tools for eco-friendly home use
  • 46-inch adjustable handle with quick thread connection for easy assembly
  • 2-in-1 wet/dry mop head for cleaning in kitchens and small spaces
  • Two bamboo fiber mop pads that capture dust and dirt effectively
  • Space-saving, compact design with one handle for both broom and mop

This eco-friendly cleaning kit is ideal for everyday maintenance in your kitchen, mudroom, or any area prone to messes. The dust mop works with both dry and wet surfaces, while the debris pan simplifies mess collection. The built-in storage rope ensures all tools remain organized and within reach.
