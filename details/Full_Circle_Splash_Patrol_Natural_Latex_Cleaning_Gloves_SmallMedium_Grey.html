Full Circle Splash Patrol Natural Latex Cleaning and Dish Gloves

Designed for comfort and functionality, these gloves are your go-to solution for all cleaning needs. With 100% all-natural latex, they offer a perfect blend of flexibility, durability, and skin-friendly care.

Key Features

  • Durable natural latex construction for long-lasting use
  • Slip-resistant grip for secure handling in wet environments
  • Soft inner lining for comfort and protection against hot water
  • Cotton cuffs to absorb moisture and keep hands dry
  • Hang-dry loops for easy and quick drying

Why Choose These Gloves?

Whether you're tackling tough stains in the kitchen or mopping up messes in the bathroom, these gloves are designed to keep your hands safe, dry, and protected. The slip-resistant exterior ensures a firm grip even when things get wet, while the cotton cuff efficiently catches excess water to prevent slip-ups.

With their premium soft inner lining, they are gentle on the skin and ideal for use with hot water. The built-in hang-dry loops make storage and maintenance a breeze, helping you keep your workspace organized and clean.

Perfect For

Homeowners, kitchen workers, and bathroom cleaners will find these gloves to be an essential tool. They are ideal for dishwashing, scrubbing countertops, cleaning surfaces, or even light mopping. Their eco-friendly materials and reliable performance make them a great choice for both everyday use and professional settings.
